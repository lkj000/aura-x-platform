**Design Movement**: Afro-Futurist Minimalism

**Core Principles**:
1. **Sonic Visualization**: The interface should feel like a visual representation of sound, with waveforms and frequencies integrated into the background and UI elements.
2. **Cultural Resonance**: Use patterns and motifs inspired by South African art and textiles, but abstracted and modernized for a digital context.
3. **Tactile Feedback**: Controls should feel physical and responsive, mimicking the feel of high-end analog gear.
4. **Focus & Flow**: The layout should minimize distractions and guide the user into a state of creative flow.

**Color Philosophy**:
- **Deep Indigo & Midnight Blue**: Representing the "Private School" and "Deep House" roots of Amapiano.
- **Vibrant Gold & Copper**: Accents representing the richness and warmth of the log drum and brass instruments.
- **Electric Purple**: For AI and generative elements, signifying the future.

**Layout Paradigm**:
- **Modular & Fluid**: A flexible grid system that allows panels to be resized and rearranged, but with a strong central focus on the timeline and piano roll.
- **Sidebar Navigation**: A persistent sidebar for quick access to tools, instruments, and settings.

**Signature Elements**:
- **Log Drum Waveform**: A stylized waveform of a log drum kick used as a loading animation and background element.
- **Patterned Borders**: Subtle geometric patterns on panel borders inspired by Ndebele art.
- **Glowing Indicators**: Status lights and meters that glow with intensity based on audio levels.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- **Direct Manipulation**: Users should be able to drag, drop, and resize elements directly.
- **Contextual Controls**: Tools and options should appear only when needed, based on the selected element.

**Animation**:
- **Smooth Transitions**: Panels should slide and fade in/out smoothly.
- **Pulse & Breathe**: UI elements should pulse gently with the beat of the music.

**Typography System**:
- **Headings**: "Space Grotesk" - A modern, geometric sans-serif with a futuristic feel.
- **Body**: "Inter" - Clean and legible for UI text.
- **Monospace**: "JetBrains Mono" - For code and technical data.
</text>

**Design Movement**: Kasi Tech Industrial

**Core Principles**:
1. **Raw & Authentic**: The design should reflect the gritty, street-level origins of Amapiano ("Kasi" style).
2. **Industrial Utility**: The interface should look like a powerful tool, with exposed structures and functional details.
3. **High Contrast**: Bold use of black and white, with neon accents for visibility.
4. **Data-Driven**: Emphasize the AI and analytics capabilities with detailed visualizations and metrics.

**Color Philosophy**:
- **Matte Black & Charcoal**: The primary background colors, creating a dark mode aesthetic.
- **Neon Green & Hot Pink**: High-visibility accents for active elements and alerts.
- **Concrete Grey**: For secondary panels and structural elements.

**Layout Paradigm**:
- **Dashboard Style**: A dense, information-rich layout with multiple panels visible at once.
- **Top Bar Navigation**: A global navigation bar for quick switching between main views.

**Signature Elements**:
- **Exposed Grid**: A visible grid background that gives a technical, blueprint-like feel.
- **Stencil Typography**: Use of stencil fonts for labels and headers, referencing industrial markings.
- **Glitch Effects**: Subtle glitch animations on hover and transitions to suggest the digital/AI nature.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- **Click & Clack**: Buttons and switches should have a satisfying "click" sound and visual feedback.
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Extensive support for keyboard shortcuts for power users.

**Animation**:
- **Snap & Pop**: Transitions should be instant and snappy, with no lag.
- **Data Flow**: Visualizations of data flowing between components.

**Typography System**:
- **Headings**: "Chakra Petch" - A square, industrial sans-serif.
- **Body**: "Roboto" - A neutral, readable sans-serif.
- **Monospace**: "Fira Code" - For code and data.

**Layout Paradigm**:
- **Circular & Radial**: Use circular layouts for menus and controls, representing the cyclical nature of rhythm.
- **Center-Stage**: The main creative canvas is always in the center, with tools radiating outwards.

- **Fractal Patterns**: Background patterns based on African fractals.
- **Organic Shapes**: Buttons and panels with rounded, organic shapes.
- **Ambient Lighting**: Soft, ambient lighting effects that change with the mood of the music.
